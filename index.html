About Serah

Built from the inside out.

"The moment I understood that my wellbeing was the foundation of my business, everything changed. That realisation is the work I now do with others."

I am a licensed counseling psychologist, Chevening Scholar, and Mandela Washington Fellow with a Master's in Business Psychology from Loughborough University, UK. I sit at the intersection of mental health and business strategy — not because it is a niche, but because I lived the cost of keeping them separate.

I work with founders expanding into the UK and Kenyan markets, professionals building international careers, and scholarship candidates who are more qualified than they know. I also lead Sema — an online mental health platform for Africans and the diaspora — and the Athea Global Foundation, advocating for mental health access across the continent.

I have been featured by Nation Media Group, Standard Media, NTV, and KTN. I am a published researcher with work focused on addiction recovery and mental health outcomes, and a speaker at international platforms including the International Women's Day Conference 2025.
